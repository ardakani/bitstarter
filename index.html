Within minutes create and distribute your restaurants menu. The UI will be wizard like and built on “type once” principle. This means that the app will keep track of all input data so that you don't have to e.g. type in the same dish that is used in different menus twice. You don't have to spend time on designing the layout just use any of our templates. Our proffesional designers can help out if you would like to create your own layout.

Find the best restaurant for the moment with the Whatscooking app. Search the menus by dish, restaurant name, location, price range, rating or a combination of any. You can also rate the dish and the restaurant after your visit. The WhatsCooking app will be available for free as web and mobile app.

GastroCloud provides API:s and widgets for easy integration with other site's or IT-systems. GastroCloud will aim for building plugins for the most common content management systems.

What is GastroCloud?
GastroCloud provides the missing tools and services to bring restaurants and guests closer. The menu app for example will make the menu maintenance more efficient for the restaurants and at the same enable our members to search for up to date information the based on their interest. A regular search could be to look for a certain dish based on the location and rating.

Nothing it's free.

Nothing it's free.

We will sell ads.
There are already many similar services online, what's different about GastroCloud?
One of the major concerns with catalogue based services like ours is to keep the data (in our case the menus) up to date. We believe that this can be achieved by building useful and easy to use applications for our different users that not only keeps our data up to date but also add some value for the user. In the menu case, we provide a menu application that will make the administration of the menu simple and fast for the restaurant owner. The information inside the menus will keep our database up to date and we can with our up to date data help our hungry members make the right choice.
